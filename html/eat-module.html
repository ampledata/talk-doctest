Test & Example

  • Eating a regulation taco: 
    >>> lunch='taco'
>>> eat(lunch)
'Eating a delicious taco for $1.'
  • Eating an engineer's food pellet for $3: 
    >>> dinner='burrito'
>>> eat(dinner, 3)
'Eating a delicious burrito for $3.'
